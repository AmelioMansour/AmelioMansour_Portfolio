Rivian Automotive

Rivian Automotive is a up and coming electric vehicle manufacturer. They are mostly known for their newly announced electric pickup truck and sporty SUV. The company just entered as a public trading company on November 10th, since then, the market cap has already surpassed 100 Billion. This gives it a market cap more than ford and GM. However,the company has not even sold over 200 cars in total. The main financial investors are Amazon and Ford, with Amazon owning 22% and Ford owning 12%. The vehicle on the right is Rivian's 2022 R1T. The electric truck has good sized range of 314 miles and starts off at around $74,000. Rivian reported that the R1T have a 0-60 of 3 seconds flat which has never been heard of for a truck.
